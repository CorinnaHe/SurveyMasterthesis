Informed Consent

You are invited to participate in an academic research study conducted as part of a Master’s thesis at a German public university.

The purpose of this study is to examine human decision-making in a financial context. You will be asked to evaluate fictional credit applications and make judgments based on the information provided. Some decisions will include support from an AI-based system.

Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time without providing a reason and without any negative consequences.

The study does not collect any directly identifying personal data. All responses are processed anonymously and used exclusively for academic research purposes in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data will be stored securely and will only be accessible to the researchers involved in this project. The data will be retained only as long as necessary for research purposes.

The study takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.

If you have questions about this study or about your data, you may contact the researcher via the contact information provided on the recruitment platform.

By continuing, you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years old
  • You have read and understood the information above
  • You voluntarily agree to participate in this study
